Frequently Asked Questions

To list your property, you first need to create an account or log in. Once logged in, navigate to the "Sell" page from the main menu. You will find a "Sell Your Property" button which will take you to a form where you can enter all the details about your property, including title, description, price, and images.

Currently, listing a property on SRIT Realty is completely free! We believe in providing a simple and accessible platform for sellers. Any changes to our fee structure will be announced well in advance.

When a potential buyer is interested in your property, they can send you a message directly from your property's detail page. You will receive these messages in your personal "Inbox," which you can access via the envelope icon in the header when you are logged in. From there, you can view conversations and reply to potential buyers.

You can manage all your listings from your "Seller Dashboard." Once logged in, click "Sell" in the navigation bar. On your dashboard, each property card will have "Edit" and "Delete" options, allowing you to update details or remove the listing entirely.
